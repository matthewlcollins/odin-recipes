Pizza Recipe!

Ingredients

  • Pizza bases
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Pizza Sauce
  • Anchovies
  • Peperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven to 200 degrees c
  2. Once oven is heated add pizza bases for 2 mins
  3. Once that time is up remove the pizza bases
  4. Add Tomato Pizza Sauce evenly around the base and then add the cheese in a similar fashion
  5. Add remaining toppings around the pizza and insert back into the oven for 10-12 mins or to your liking
  6. Remove pizza, allow to cool. Cut into slices and enjoy
